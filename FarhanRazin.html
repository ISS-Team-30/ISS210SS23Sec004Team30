Effect on health due to processed Food

Posted by Farhan Masum Razin on April 28, 2023

Foods that are processed have a negative impact on person’s health and lifestyle.

Processing foods can sometimes be beneficial but lately most of the processed foods are harmful to our health.

Processed foods have an increased amount of sugar, salt and fat.

Processed foods are popular because they are cheap, and are ready made so a person does not have to spend time cooking.

As it is mentioned in the article, processed food can cause cardiovascular disease.

Cardiovascular disease is related to the heart and blood vessels.

Blood level reduce due to buildup of fatty acids in the artery of the heart, can have a severe impact on a person’s health.

As, there is an increased amount of fat, sugar, and salt in processed foods they can easily help build fatty acids in a person’s artery.

As mentioned in the video, processed food can make a person gain weight, reduce the hormones that make you feel full and increase the hormones that makes you feel hungry.

Processed food can be highly addictive and highly palatable as you never get tired of eating it.

Processed foods also contains a lot of chemicals which are the main factor for all these harm. Switching to whole food that are locally sourced, and contains no chemicals and preservatives can be a good alternative to processed foods.
